Frequently Asked Questions

How do I take the screening test?
Navigate to the Screening page. The test consists of 10 simple Yes/No questions about your memory and daily activities. It takes less than 2 minutes to complete.
Is this a medical diagnosis?
No. ReMind is a screening tool designed to track changes over time. It is not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. If your score indicates changes, we recommend consulting a doctor.
How do I share results with my doctor or family?
Go to the My Caretaker page. You can enter the email address of a trusted family member or doctor. You can choose to automatically send them your weekly summaries.
Is my data private?
Yes. Your screening results are stored locally on your device or encrypted if you create an account. We do not sell your personal health data to third parties.
